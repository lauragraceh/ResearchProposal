An Introduction to This Project

Introduction, Mar 9, 2022

I am examining the issue of how public art exists in a digital space, and how we interact with it in that space. This topic falls under the generatilized area of art history and anthropology. It is important to this general area because, by accounting for social media/digital spaces, it acts as an update to existing theories about public art
